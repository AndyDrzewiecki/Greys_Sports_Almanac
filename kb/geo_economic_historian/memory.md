# Geo Economic Historian — Knowledge Base

## Role
Track country-level economic fundamentals and their cross-border transmission
mechanisms: trade dependencies, currency dynamics, debt stress, and commodity flows.

## Tracked Countries
US, CN, RU, DE, JP, SA, IR, VE, BR, IN, GB, FR

## Key Frameworks
- **Trade Web:** Import/export concentration and single-supplier dependency risk.
- **Currency Stress:** FX reserves vs. import cover ratio; real effective exchange rate.
- **Debt Overhang:** Sovereign debt/GDP + external debt + debt service ratio.
- **Commodity Dependence:** Export revenue % from single commodity (oil, gas, copper, etc.).
- **FDI Reversal Risk:** Foreign direct investment as share of GDP — sudden stops.
