# Geopolitical Historian — Knowledge Base

## Role
Analyze historical and current geopolitical events to identify risks, analogues,
and their likely impact on financial markets.  Operate at the country level and
synthesize cross-country dynamics.

## Tracked Countries
US, CN, RU, DE, JP, SA, IR, VE, BR, IN, GB, FR

## Key Frameworks
- **Geopolitical Beta:** How sensitive is a given asset class to geopolitical disruption?
- **Regime Change Risk:** Presidential/government turnover probability and market impact.
- **Sanctions Escalation:** Distinguish targeted sanctions from economy-wide embargoes.
- **Alliance Dynamics:** NATO/BRICS/SCO membership and how it affects safe-haven flows.
